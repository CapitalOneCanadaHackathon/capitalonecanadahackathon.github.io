The Digital for Good Summit empowers charities and non-profit organizations to use technology as a force for good.

Following the Digital for Good Tech Jam, this event further supports charities as they embrace new possibilities. We’re looking forward to giving participants access to tech leaders and facilitating collaboration among peers. Whether your goal is to build a tech-focused team, to learn about new available tools or to spark inspiration, we have something for you!


Cost: FREE

The Capital One Digital for Good Summit is an event exclusively for registered charities.
