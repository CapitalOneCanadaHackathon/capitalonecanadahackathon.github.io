About Gift the Code

Gathering our collective strengths. Use digital for good.

We’re back! This year’s hackathon will be even bigger and better than last year’s – and last year’s event was incredible.

We’re taking coders, data scientists, designers and other bright people, putting them together in a great space, serving up a hearty helping of tech-based challenges and letting it all simmer together for a couple of days.

The best part: it’s all for charity. You’ll be using your super powers for good by creating functional, usable tech solutions for organizations who work tirelessly to help others and make the world a kinder place. What’s better than that?
