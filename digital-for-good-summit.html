Digital for Good Summit 2018

06-20-18

With 74% of Canadian charities feeling that their organization is threatened by a growing tech gap, Capital One is on a mission to enable non-profits to thrive in a digital future. Through our work with Gift the Code, we realized the increasing need to support charities as they move into an ever-changing world.

In June 2018, our first-ever Capital One Digital for Good Summit brought together over 130 charity representatives and Canada’s top tech talent as they worked together to bridge the growing tech gap. Speakers included experts from Facebook and the Globe and Mail Content Studio, as well as representatives from charities like Kids Help Phone and Imagine Canada – among others!

Check out more information on our Digital for Good Summit.
