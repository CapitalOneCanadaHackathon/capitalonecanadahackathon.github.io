#UseDigitalForGood

Our DNA is Digital

This year’s Gift the Code is going to be a smash! We’re hosting our third annual hackathon at the new, beautiful Globe and Mail Centre in downtown Toronto.

Since 2016, the annual Gift the Code Hackathon has had a simple purpose at its core: to help our community by leveraging our collective skills and use digital for good to help bridge the tech gap faced by local charities.

We bring together coders, developers, designers, coaches, mentors and many more smart people, and we pair them up with charities. We’re looking for a diverse group of keeners with big brains and even bigger hearts. Everyone works together over an intensive weekend to solve a problem and have an amazing time. The challenges are positive, the end result is useable, open-source code that can be used by anyone, and we’re all in good company.

Join in on the Fun!

Join in on the Fun!

We’re looking for developers, data scientists, designers, academics and advocates to #UseDigitalForGood to solve our charity partners’ challenges. All experience levels are welcome as we crowdsource innovation!

The Capital One Digital for Good Promise

Capital One is a data-driven technology company and we believe that sharing our collective skills and our digital expertise can help Canadian charities bridge a growing tech gap. Helping charities thrive in a digital future will bring their services to a wider cross-section of people more effectively – building stronger communities where we live and work.

Our DNA is digital, and we’re using digital for good.

To learn more about what we do to drive social impact, check out our Digital For Good Summit.
